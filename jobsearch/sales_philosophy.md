# Sales Philosophy — Lukas Larsson

## Background
Started in technical B2B sales at Polarpumpen (BHG subsidiary), progressing through 
business development, product management and technical sales at Svensk 
Installationspartner, BHG Group, and Valeryd AB. Recently reached final stage interview 
for Sales Engineer role at Spirax Sarco, validating market fit for technical sales profiles.

## Operational Approach
Attentive to customer needs and underlying business processes to identify opportunities 
to save time, reduce costs, or generate new revenue. Uses SPIN methodology:

- **Situation**: What does the current situation look like?
- **Problem**: What problems are you experiencing?
- **Impact**: What impact does the problem have on the business?
- **Need/Benefit**: How can our solution solve this?

## Goals & Systems
Believes in clear goals backed by systems and processes. Example: to close 7 deals at 
1.5M SEK each requires meeting 70 potential customers, which requires a systematic 
outreach strategy — not just activity.

## ROI Focus
Works with data and customer ROI as the primary value driver. Keeps it simple and 
ensures the numbers tell the story directly.

## Relationship Building
Values long-term customer relationships through regular check-ins, active listening and 
market input to stay ahead of customer needs.
